## 💡 Key Learnings

### Identity & Scaling
- **Insight:** Folder-slug based IDs are fragile and prone to collision in a sharded documentation system.
- **Takeaway:** Pivoting to a path-based unique identifier (`projectSlug:fileStem`) during Epic 8.3 was a critical stabilization move that unblocked reliable document lookups.

### Acceptance Criteria Alignment
- **Insight:** Natural language specs can lose nuance when translated into formal AC, leading to implementation mismatches (e.g., CTA behavior).
- **Takeaway:** The team must treat AC as a "living contract." If implementation feels misaligned with the vision, the AC should be updated immediately rather than assuming the original text is perfect.

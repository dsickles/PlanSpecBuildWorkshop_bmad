## Technique Execution Results

**Analogical Thinking:**

- **Interactive Focus:** Explored analogies for the portfolio structure, moving from a museum exhibit to a robust builder's workshop.
- **Key Breakthroughs:** 
  - **[Exhibit Design]: The "Plan Spec Build Workshop" Pipeline.** Structuring the portfolio into three clear zones: the Agent Studio (the tools/frameworks), Blueprints (rigorous planning documents like PRDs, architecture, and test plans), and the Build Lab (the prototype/code). 
  - **[Agent Studio]: The Apprenticeship Journey.** Framing the use of off-the-shelf tools (like BMAD) not as magic, but as an apprenticeship with powerful workshop tools, demonstrating the operator's skill and methodology.
  - **[Blueprint Archive]: The Discipline of Planning.** Emphasizing the *process* of agentic building through Test Plans, Trade-off Logs, and explicit constraint mapping, separating a professional PM's approach from amateur "vibe coding."
- **User Creative Strengths:** Strong focus on practical utility, clear audience targeting (non-technical peers/managers), and a highly developed BS-detector for industry jargon ("no fluff").
- **Energy Level:** Highly engaged, driving the analogy toward a grounded, professional standard.
